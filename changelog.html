Message Log Privacy & Media

v4.4.0 — latest
March 2026

Major upgrades to the message logger — full privacy controls, rich media rendering for videos and audio, a complete pinned messages system with canvas views, and a smarter canvas engine that trims blank space automatically.

Privacy Controls

  • .mlog privacy — view and toggle all privacy settings per channel
  • encrypt — AES-256 encrypts message content and author names stored in JSON
  • devmode — log images sent to your DMs instead of the channel
  • owneronly — restricts all mlog commands to server admins and owner
  • autounpin — removes pins from Discord after storing them in mlog

Pinned Messages

  • .mlog pins — canvas image of pinned messages (8 per page, filter by user)
  • .mlog focuspin <n> — single pin as a focused canvas image
  • .mlog focuspins @user — all pins from one user as a canvas image
  • .mlog mpin — manually add any message to pins by replying to it
  • Pins auto-fetch on .mlog on and refresh when channel pins change

Media Rendering

  • MP4 and video attachments render as Discord-style cards with thumbnail and play button
  • Video thumbnails fetched via Discord CDN, with ffmpeg fallback for older videos
  • Audio attachments render as Discord-style player cards with filename and timeline
  • Audio duration fetched via ffprobe directly from the CDN URL
  • Canvas crops to actual content height — no more blank space at the bottom

Criminal Justice System

v4.3.0
March 2026

A full crime and punishment economy. Rob enough times and you become a wanted criminal with escalating penalties. Fund the police, report criminals, and watch the streets get cleaned up — or don't, and risk everything.

Criminal Levels

  • Each rob increases your criminal level (max 5) — earnings penalties stack per level
  • Lv1 Wanted: -30%, 7d | Lv2 Felon: -40%, 14d | Lv3 Fugitive: -50%, 21d
  • Lv4 Public Enemy: -60%, 28d | Lv5 Police Target: -70%, permanent until caught
  • Penalties apply to daily, work, and beg — shown every time you earn

Police Catch System

  • Catch chance per rob scales with police fund — 0: ~3%, 1M: ~33%, 1T: ~63%, 1Q: ~78%
  • Lv1-2 bust: wallet seized | Lv3-5 bust: wallet + bank + tokens all seized
  • Police patrol random NPC criminals periodically — seized coins go to the fund
  • Top donor gets credited in every arrest announcement

.report & .fund police

  • .report @user — costs 3 tokens, recovers all stolen coins, triggers immediate police bust
  • Bonus tokens rewarded on report (1 per rob by that person, max 5)
  • .fund police <amount> — donate coins, earn +2% work bonus per 1B donated (max +30%)
  • Rob protection can't shield you from people you've already robbed

Event Cards & Polish

v4.2.5
March 2026

Weekly rotating event cards with animated drops, fused card durability drain, a reworked rob system that scales with rebirth, profile card emoji icons, and a round of bug fixes.

Weekly Event Cards

  • A new event card rotates every Monday — different card each week
  • 8% chance to drop during any .cardroll if you haven't claimed it this week
  • Event drops use the fusion GIF animation — you'll know when it hits
  • Event cards never lose durability — permanent once obtained
  • .card event — shows the current week's card, rotation timer, and whether you own it

Fused Card Durability

  • Fused cards lose durability each time their bonus activates in gameplay
  • Drain per use: Mythical -0.15%, Legendary/Rare/Uncommon -0.05%, Common -0.02%
  • At 0% the card auto re-rolls into a fresh copy of the same tier
  • .cards limited shows remaining durability % for every fused card

Rob Overhaul

  • Max rob amount scales with your rebirth — R0: 100K, R10: ~3B, R20: ~100T, R24: ~6Q
  • Success chance scales off rebirth gap vs your target — ±2% per level, capped at 40–95%
  • Steal card bonuses now boost your success chance in addition to the stolen amount
  • Catch penalty scales with your rebirth (30% base, +1%/level, max 60%)

Profile & Fixes

  • Profile card stat icons replaced — colored dots swapped for Twemoji PNG images (⚡ 💬 🪙 🏦 🎫)
  • Wallet token field now shows a 🎫 icon instead of a bare T abbreviation
  • Number formatting was showing spaces as separators on Linux — fixed globally
  • .cards burn stops giving rolls after 3 burns — dust still always awarded
