Economy Overhaul

latest
February 2026

A sweeping overhaul of core economy and gambling systems — fixing broken mechanics, adding real risk/reward, and introducing pets, job levels, and Arena ELO.

Gambling Fixes

  • Slots — weighted rarity, fixed pair detection
  • Coinflip — pick heads or tails before flipping
  • Blackjack — 3:2 natural BJ, upfront bet, double down
  • Spin — legendary tier now reachable (~1%), rarity label

Mines & Ladders

  • Mines — multiplierBoost applied, bonuses on cashout
  • Mines — 5-minute idle timeout forfeits bet
  • Ladders — left/right choice now affects success odds
  • Rob — 70% success chance, catch penalty added

New Systems

  • Pet system — adopt, train, battle, release
  • Job levels — 10 tiers with XP and multiplier bonuses
  • Arena ELO — persistent ratings after every battle
  • Fish encyclopedia — .fishbook with seasonal bonuses

Quality of Life

  • Leaderboard — total wealth (wallet + bank), rebirth tag
  • Leaderboard — fixed box-drawing borders
  • Cooldowns survive bot restarts
  • Removed stock market system

Commands

  • .adopt / .mypet / .trainpet / .petbattle
  • .job — view job level and XP progress
  • .arenaelo / .arenalb
  • .fishbook — fish encyclopedia

Web Marketplace

Autofishing

Commands

Fishing Overhaul

v3.3.0
February 2026

Major fishing system overhaul — mythical tier fish, random cast events, streak bonuses, bait consumables, and a fully reworked stat display.

New Fish & Tiers

  • Mythical tier — 4 ultra-rare fish (5M–50M coins)
  • 2 new legendary fish added
  • 4 new rare fish (Void Eel, Abyssal Angler...)
  • 8 new common/uncommon fish

Cast Events

  • Double catch — reel in two at once
  • Treasure — find bonus coins on the line
  • Lucky cast — rare chance doubled that throw
  • Escape, storm, shark, junk events

Streak & Bait

  • Streak system — up to 3× value at 100 casts
  • Bait consumables (Worm → Cosmic Bait)
  • .fishshop buy <bait> to purchase bait
  • All 55 rods now have rare-chance scaling

Commands

  • .fish — cast with full event system
  • .fishshop — browse fish + bait page
  • .fishshop buy <bait>
  • .fishstats — full stat breakdown

Commands

  • .diceduel decline

Details

  • 30s cooldown between rounds
  • Visual dice roll results
